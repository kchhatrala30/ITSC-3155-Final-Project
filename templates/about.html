All about NUTT


Welcome to the NUTT!

Members of Niner Nation fight the constant struggle to find a clean and acessible restroom around our wonderful campus. That's why Niners United Toilet Tracking (aka NUTT) is committed to helping you find a clean and accessible restroom depending on your needs.

Here, you can view all of the restroom ratings on the UNC Charlotte campus! And, if there isn't a rating for a certain restroom, sign up or login and be the first to rate that restroom. For access to a plethora of other nutty features, such as the NUTT Map, join the NUTT today!

Why should I join the NUTT?

Joining the NUTT allows you to voice your opinion by allowing you to post restroom ratings. Along with posting, you can also leave comments with your thoughts, such as whether you agree or disagree with the rating. If you're in a rush, you can simply upvote or downvote a rating or comment, and you can also access the NUTT Map if logged in. Join the NUTT today!

What makes NUTT unique?

What makes NUTT unique is the NUTT Map, which can be accessed under the "Maps" tab on our site. The NUTT Map, with your permission, tracks your current location and determines which building on campus you are closest to.

You may ask, what does our dear NUTT Map do with such trivial info? After determining the building closest to you, NUTT Map will show you all the ratings tagged with that building, so you know the cleanest spot to go!
